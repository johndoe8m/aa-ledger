Check a Day
⌄
Pull the "1 Day" Total Fee from ClubGG's Club Data screen for a given date and drop it in here. It checks that against everything you've already logged for that date and tells you if you're over or under.
Float Check
⌄
Enter what you're actually holding in real money right now, plus Total Member Chips from ClubGG's Counter screen. This tells you if you're covered or short — do it weekly and a gap gets caught small instead of building up. The buffer accounts for normal swings from players winning against other union clubs before settlement clears — it won't flag those as a problem.
IOUs
⌄
Money someone owes you (or you owe someone) that isn't in your accounts yet. Keeps it visible without it getting counted as real money until it actually lands — update your Float Check numbers once it's actually paid.
Savings Goal
⌄
Set a target and it stays locked until you hit it. Optionally peel off a % of your cut automatically every time new rake comes in, or drop money in by hand.
